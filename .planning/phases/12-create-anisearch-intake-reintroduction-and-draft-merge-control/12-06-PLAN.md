<objective>
Close three UAT-diagnosed bugs in the Phase 12 create-route AniSearch flow.

Purpose: Live UAT with AniSearch IDs 6123 and 5468 revealed: (1) the create success path cancels its own redirect timeout by resetting staged-asset state, (2) the 409 Conflict response from the backend is thrown as an ApiError instead of being parsed as a redirect payload, and (3) incoming "Sequel" graph edges are silently dropped by the relation-label mapper, causing 0 relation matches for animes that are sequels/spin-offs of existing DB entries.
Output: Three targeted fixes — each a few lines — with regression tests proving each behavior.
</objective>
