<objective>
Implement the Team4s-owned fansub membership model on top of authenticated app users: group assignment, `fansub_lead` storage, and the admin MVP to inspect and manage those assignments.

Purpose: After Phase 43 authenticates users, Team4s still needs a clean way to say which app user belongs to which fansub group and who leads that group, without leaking those business roles into Keycloak or older contributor-only tables. This plan establishes only the minimal durable membership and lead-assignment seams needed for Phase 44 authz consumption; richer member-management workflows belong to Phase 45.
</objective>
