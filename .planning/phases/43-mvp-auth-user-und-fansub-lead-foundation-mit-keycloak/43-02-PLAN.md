<objective>
Complete the authenticated app-user seam after the Keycloak foundation exists: finalize `CurrentUser`, wire frontend login/logout/session behavior, and add the first protected admin-user APIs governed by Team4s `platform_admin`.

Purpose: Phase 43 only becomes a usable foundation once a real browser session can authenticate through Keycloak, the backend can resolve a stable app principal across the runtime, and platform admins can inspect the registered user base without falling back to the old fixed-admin shortcut. This plan adopts and hardens the seams introduced in 43-01; it should not re-open infrastructure or schema-foundation scope unless the upstream baseline is insufficient.
</objective>
